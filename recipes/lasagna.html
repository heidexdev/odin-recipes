Lasagna

Description

Here’s a short and simple recipe for classic lasagna.

Ingridients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles (no-boil or regular)
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 cups marinara or meat sauce
  • 1 egg
  • Salt, pepper, fresh basil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). If using regular noodles, boil until al dente; drain.
  2. Mix filling: In a bowl, combine ricotta, 1 cup mozzarella, Parmesan, egg, salt, and pepper.
  3. Layer in a baking dish:
    • Thin layer of sauce
    • 3 noodles
    • 1/3 ricotta mixture
    • Sauce
    • Repeat two more times.
  4. Top with remaining mozzarella.
  5. Cover with foil (spray with oil to prevent sticking) and bake 25 minutes. Uncover and bake 10–15 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  6. Rest for 10 minutes before slicing

Enjoy your lasagna!
